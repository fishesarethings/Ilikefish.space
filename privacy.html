Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how fishesarethings (the operator of ilikefish.space) collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you visit or use the website. The site provides browser-based mini-games and related content. This site is largely static and stores most user-related game data locally in your browser (LocalStorage / IndexedDB).

Data We Collect

  • Local browser data: Game progress, preferences and other non-personal data stored locally in localStorage and IndexedDB.
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics (Google Analytics).
  • Advertising: Data collected by ad networks (Google AdSense and Ezoic).
  • Server logs: Standard logs by the hosting provider (GitHub Pages / Netlify).

How We Use Data

  • To provide and operate the site, including loading games and resources.
  • To store game progress and preferences locally.
  • To analyze site usage (aggregated, anonymized analytics).
  • To serve and manage ads (Google AdSense and Ezoic).

Cookies & Local Storage

Cookies and local storage may be used for analytics, ads, and saving your progress. You can clear them through your browser settings. Export/import features are provided to let you manage your local game data directly.
